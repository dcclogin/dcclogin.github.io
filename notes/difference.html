Incohérences et Différences

  • Explore the possibility of “bootstrapping” Logik and Mathematics in the spirit of Wissenschaft der Logik.
  • Explore the possibility of formalizing “Logics” that are alien to formal logicians, mathematicians, PL people.
  • Re-search the “pre-history” of formal logic and mathematics in the spirit of Das Kapital.
  • Build bridges to our pioneers’ theories and praxis including natural sciences like quantum; others like politics.
