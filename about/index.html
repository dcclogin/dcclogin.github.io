I am a D.Sc. student in the Department of Computer Science at Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering (SICE), Indiana University Bloomington (IUB). I once majored in Bioinformatics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). My interests range from theoretical aspects of programming languages to continental philosophy (Phenomenology as well as other descendants of German Idealism). I have a strong interest to reconcile mathematics, formal logique, and programming languages with continental philosophy. I read Jean-Yves Girard (logique), Alain Badiou & Slavoj Žižek (philosophies).
